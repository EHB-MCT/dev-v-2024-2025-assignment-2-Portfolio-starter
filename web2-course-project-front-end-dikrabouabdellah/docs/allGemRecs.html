This month's GemRec: Uramichi Oniisan

What is a GemRec?

Uramichi Oniisan or Life lessons with Uramichi won the titel this month for it's rare humor and plot.

Came out in the summer of 2021 it is surprising to see how little people know about this anime. The anime talks about the life of a retired gymnast who is now an actor in a kids tv-show. Showing the daily struggles of a man in his thirties doing a job he hates while still being humerous.

This anime is garanteed to make you laugh, and has the well-deserved title of GemRec of September 2023.
