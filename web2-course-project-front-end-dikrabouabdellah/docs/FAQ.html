What is a GemRec?

A GemRec is a recommendation that I give every month. In order for it to be a GemRec it has to be underrated and really good. Under the category All GemRecs you will find the GemRecs of the previous months. No need to look up the term on Google because I invented it.

Can I send recommendations?

Of course! You can directly send me a message on this email adress dikradikra996@gmail.com, however this is no garantee that your anime will be on my website. I'll have to review it an take it into consideration.

Why can't I find popular anime?

In this website you'll only find underrated anime or hidden gems. Popular animes have already enough light shining on, time to open you mind for new posibilities, and what better place than in GemRecs.
